Maher Zein

"Forgive Me"

Forgive Me is the second studio album by Swedish Muslim singer-songwriter, Maher Zain, was released on April 1, 2012 by Awakening Records. The album was a commercially success and has sold over 100,000 copies (7× platinum) in Malaysia as of June 2016.

The first music video from the album was titled 'Freedom'. It was released on March 27, 2011 by Malaysian director, Mohd Hafizi bin Mat Khalib. The music video was filmed on February 25 & 26, 2011 at Malawati Stadium, Malaysia.[citation needed] The second music video from the album was 'Number One For Me'. It was released on March 15, 2012. The video was directed by Mike Harris, who had previously directed 'Insha Allah' from the album Thank You Allah.[3] 'Number One For Me' was released on iTunes earlier on March 13, 2012. Performers in the video include child actor Massimo Loreti.[citation needed] The third music video entitled 'So Soon', was released on June 3, 2012. It was directed by Mike Harris.[4] The fourth music video was released on January 9, 2014, titled Muhammad (pbuh)
